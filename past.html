About My Past

Talking about my past experience in terms of computing, I did not really have any chance to learn about computer science or programming. In Vietnamese High schools, what we were mostly taught in the IT subject was Microsoft Office and skills to proficiently using Word, Excel or PowerPoint. Despite that, in grade 12, while considering my future pathway, I was really interested in the IT industry and looking into what skills were needed. I then started studying programming myself, and the first language I chose was Java. The only resource I got and followed at that moment was tutorial videos on YouTube. However, it was a great start as I did some coding myself and it felt really cool to do so, though they were just simple exercises. After that, I continued my self-learning sessions and I also learnt a bit more about Python too. So up until officially landed some IT subjects at UTS, I do have some basic understanding in terms of computing.
